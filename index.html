Play Pig Dice!

Pig Dice is a dice game where players take turns repeatedly rolling a die to compete in points. For each turn they can roll until they decide to "hold", increasing their total score by that amount. If a "1" is rolled, they score nothing for that turn and their turn ends. The first player to score or more points wins!
